I am a Computational Social Scientist. Much of my research has been about social networks and the social impacts of online technology. My current work focuses on the use of large scale social network data to examine national and inter-national cultural behavior. I also work on techniques and methods for web application development and architecture, data collection and visualization.

Most of my publications and working papers are available on this site, along with information about teaching, CS and Social Research resources and occasional writing. My Curriculum Vitae has more detail.
